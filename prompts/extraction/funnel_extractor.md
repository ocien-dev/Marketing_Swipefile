# Funnel Extractor

Use `prompts/extraction/base_insight_extraction.md` as the output contract.

Focus on extracting funnel intelligence:

- acquisition;
- lead capture;
- quiz;
- webinar;
- VSL;
- checkout;
- upsell;
- downsell;
- remarketing;
- email or WhatsApp follow-up;
- launch vs evergreen;
- retention path.

Prioritize insights that help:

- CMO agent;
- COO agent;
- gestor de projetos;
- gestor de trafego;
- head de lancamentos;
- criador de quizzes.

Special rules:

- Classify sequence-based insights as `playbook_step` or `framework`.
- Preserve operational dependencies when mentioned.
- If the segment includes metrics or stage logic, include them in the insight.
