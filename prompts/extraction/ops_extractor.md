# Ops Extractor

Use `prompts/extraction/base_insight_extraction.md` as the output contract.

Focus on extracting operational and management intelligence:

- team structure;
- routines;
- production cadence;
- project management;
- campaign operations;
- creative testing workflow;
- handoffs;
- decision rituals;
- QA;
- hiring;
- dashboards;
- process bottlenecks.

Prioritize insights that help:

- COO agent;
- gestor de projetos;
- CMO agent;
- head de lancamentos;
- gestor de trafego.

Special rules:

- Classify repeatable procedures as `checklist` or `playbook_step`.
- Classify team/process structures as `framework`.
- Preserve concrete cadence, owner, and artifact names when mentioned.
