## Job

Use this module to make a VSL, offer, ad, low-ticket product, or quiz explain
why the promise is possible, why the audience should believe it now, and why
the next step is the logical continuation of that belief.

## Retrieval Scope

Retrieve curated insights with `process-mecanismo-big-idea`. When the output is
a real process skill, combine this tag with the primary process tag.

## Playbook Claims

1. A mechanism is a causal bridge from the buyer's current state to the desired
   future state; it is not just a clever name. [insight:mCaFyZpXJdE-v2-0003]
2. The mechanism must be logical and credible enough for the market context,
   especially when the claim is sensitive or "white". [insight:L7u7r6rOl68-v2-0007]
3. A single belief should guide the whole persuasion path; if each funnel step
   sells a different belief, the process skill should flag incoherence.
   [insight:mCaFyZpXJdE-v2-0004]
4. In quiz flows, the mechanism must match the promise created by the creative
   and by the questions; otherwise the quiz feels like a broken sales page.
   [insight:TOW0sWhPaZw-v2-0002]
5. In quiz-to-mini-VSL flows, the mini VSL should prove the "how" after the
   quiz has raised awareness and personalized the problem.
   [insight:TOW0sWhPaZw-v2-0012]
6. In VSL work, guide belief before asking for the purchase; mechanism and
   story should prepare the buying logic before the CTA.
   [insight:zoChfFHnlOQ-v2-0008]
7. In low-ticket offers, the promise must fit the perceived value and delivery
   format; a mechanism that implies a larger transformation than the product
   can support raises claim risk. [insight:mCaFyZpXJdE-v2-0011]
8. VSL mechanism patterns can transfer into low-ticket, quiz, mini VSL, and
   creative work, but transfer the principle rather than copying the format.
   [insight:TOW0sWhPaZw-v2-0005]

## Application Checklist

- Name the buyer's current belief or stuck state.
- State the new belief or causal explanation.
- Connect the mechanism to the promise, not only to a feature.
- Verify whether the mechanism explains the next action: watch, click, answer,
  buy, or request.
- Mark unsupported mechanism claims as `[generic-practice]` or remove them.

## When Not To Use

- Do not invent a mechanism when the curated pack only supports proof, pricing,
  delivery, or traffic tactics.
- Do not rename a generic feature as a mechanism without causal logic.
- Do not let mechanism claims outrun evidence or product delivery.
